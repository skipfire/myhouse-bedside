Low-level e-paper driving code for the CrowPanel 5.79" display is taken from Elecrow's
official ESP32-S3 sample (partial refresh), maintained in:

  https://github.com/Elecrow-RD/ESP32_S3-Ink-Screen

Vendored files (under components/bedside_status/):
  EPD.cpp, EPD.h, EPD_Init.cpp, EPD_Init.h, EPDfont.h, spi.cpp

Modified for ESPHome:
  spi.h — GPIO numbers are runtime-configurable (extern ints + defaults).
